PB01 — Version 01

Photo Booth
V01

Your moments. Instantly printed. Privately kept.

PB01 is a standalone photo booth kiosk engineered for events and high-traffic venues. Step in front of the lens, choose your frame, and walk away with a studio-quality 4x6 print in seconds. Every image is processed on-device and automatically erased after printing.

  • 4x6 inch prints — high-quality dye-sublimation output with vivid color
  • Frame selection — themed borders and layouts selectable per event
  • Auto local deletion — all captured images wiped from storage post-print
  • Touch-driven workflow — intuitive on-screen UI, no app or account required
  • Premium wood-finish enclosure — compact footprint, event-ready design

PP01 — Version 01

Printing
Point V01

Upload. Verify. Print. Done.

PP01 is a document printing kiosk built for offices, campuses, and public spaces. Upload remotely, confirm specs, pay, receive an OTP. Walk up to the machine, enter the code — it greets you by name — and printing begins immediately.

  • Remote upload — send documents from any internet-connected device to the machine
  • OTP authentication — one-time password tied to your job; machine shows your name
  • Specification preview — review page count, paper size, and cost before paying
  • A4 format — optimized for standard document sizes; black-and-white in V01
  • Integrated payment — pay directly at the kiosk before print is released
